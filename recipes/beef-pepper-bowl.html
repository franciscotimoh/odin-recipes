Beef Pepper & Egg over Rice

Ignore the soup in the back.

Beef pepper saute with 2-3 eggs over rice.

Around 80g of protein per meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Serrano peppers
  • Minced garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Korean cooking wine
  • Tuna fish sauce
  • Eggs
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Mince serrano peppers into small pieces
  2. Start cooking ground beef on medium heat and break up meat
  3. Add serrano peppers and garlic until it begins to brown
  4. Add soy sauce, cooking wine, and tuna fish sauce
  5. Saute till thoroughly cooked
  6. Serve on top of cooked rice
  7. Fry 2-3 eggs, serve on top, and enjoy
